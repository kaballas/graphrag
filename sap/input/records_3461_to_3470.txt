Title: Metric-based SLI monitors
Description: AvS introduces new evaluation types based on push testers that can be used to specify metric-based SLI monitors. Ypu're now able to push value, ratio, and good/total event occurrence metrics. You can map the metric measurements with a ruleset to states such as UP, DOWN, CRITICAL and TROUBLE. Additionally, you can configure ratio and event occurence metrics as the basis for availability calculations, such as success rate.
