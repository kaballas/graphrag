Title: Notifications and Renewal of Expiring IdP Certificates
Description: As the expiration date of an IdP certificate approaches, administrators and security administrators will receive email notifications. You can use the new Renew Certificate button to initiate the renewal. If not renewed before its expiration, the certificate will automatically be renewed by the system. Renewed certificates must be manually configured on the IdP. Also, IdP certificate renewals will appear in the Security log.
