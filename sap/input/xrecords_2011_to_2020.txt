--- Record 2018 ---
Component: SAP Build Work Zone, advanced edition
Environment: Cloud Foundry
Title: Support Passive Certificate
Description: You can define a passive certificate in the SAML Trusted IDP screen.  The purpose of adding a passive certificate is to have a backup certificate in case the active certificate has expired, and to avoid system downtime.  After adding a passive certificate in the SAP BTP cockpit, administrators must manually copy the certificate into the Administration Console - Authentication & Authorization -  SAML Trusted IDPs.  For more information, see SAML Trusted IDPs.
