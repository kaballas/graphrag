--- Record 3948 ---
Component: Cloud Identity Services
Environment: Other
Title: Root Certificate Replacement
Description: DigiCert has deprecated their DigiCert Global Root CA and will stop issuing certificates for SAP under any of its Intermediate CAs (ICA) at the end of 2023. For more information, see DigiCert root and intermediate CA certificate updates 2023.  SAP Cloud Identity Services switches to the G2 ICA and will deploy certificates signed by the new CA for the domains listed below starting in mid-November 2023. This means that the DigiCert Root CA domain certificate will be signed by DigiCert Global Root G2 instead of DigiCert Global Root CA. The following domains are affected:    *.accounts.ondemand.com      *.accounts.cloud.sap      *.accounts.sapcloud.cn      *.trial-accounts.ondemand.com      Action: If your SAP Cloud Identity Services tenants are running on any of the affected domains, ensure that you trust the new root CA: DigiCert Global Root G2.
