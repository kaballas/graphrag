--- Record 2454 ---
Component: Cloud Identity Services
Environment: Cloud Foundry,Kyma,Neo
Title: Condition and skip update - changed behavior
Description: As of September 24, 2024, Identity Provisioning will change the behavior of target transformations that contain a combination of condition and skip operations for updates. For example: Code Syntax  "condition": "$.groups empty false",
"skipOperations": ["update"],   Currently, the skip operation is evaluated before the condition. If any users or groups have been updated in the source system, they are marked as skipped in the logs, and Identity Provisioning does not proceed to evaluate the condition. As a result, users or groups that do not meet the condition are not deleted as they should be.  Following the change, the condition will be evaluated first. Users and groups who do not match the condition will be skipped. After the condition is evaluated, the skip operation will determine whether the remaining users will be skipped or provisioned.  See Transformation Expressions.
Action: Info only
Lifecycle: General Availability
Type: Announcement
