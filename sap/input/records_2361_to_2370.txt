--- Record 2368 ---
Component: Cloud Identity Services
Environment: Cloud Foundry,Kyma,Neo
Title: Separation of Signing Key for SAML and OIDC Tokens
Description: Before this release, our SAML and OpenID Connect (OIDC) implementations shared the same signing key at the tenant level. With this release, the keys for SAML and OIDC are separated which enables you to rotate signing keys for one protocol without impacting the other protocol.  For more information about tenant configuration, see:      Tenant SAML 2.0 Configurations      Tenant OpenID Connect Configurations
